Research Details

2023 - Present

Graduate Student at Nanjing Normal University

  • By utilizing model internal integration, Laplace posterior parameter estimation, and temperature coefficient decay to re-quantify uncertainty at the intermediate exits of dynamic neural networks, we achieved reliable activity recognition.
  • By utilizing the Product of Experts and carefully designed correction factors for post-processing the predicted logits at the exits of dynamic neural networks, we alleviated the overthinking phenomenon.
